Apple Developer's Group SRM


Apple Developers Group SRM is a student-run professional technical group based at SRM University, Kattankulathur. The group strives toward our mission to develop the existing technologies and bridge the technical gap between the community and the evolving technologies through various events, projects and series. We as a team follow a culture of inclusion which helps individuals develop themselves across various domains and explore their skills.





Our Mission

To create a strong student community that is ready to pick up challenges and devise innovative solutions to solve them.

Our Vision

To create an environment where one gains comprehensive learning and confidence to face the world.

Our Domains

We as a team work together but to be able to reach out to you in a much efficient manner, our team members work under one of the three domains:

Creatives

Creatives
The Creatives domain serves as the skin of the club. With their magical wands and creative minds, the members of this domain present the amazing visuals that catches everyones attention.

Corporate

Corporate
The Corporate domain serves as the nervous system of the club. Using their analytical data and strategic thinking, the members of this domain try to figure out the needs of the society and cater to them.

Technical

Creatives
